HotLinks Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

HotLinks ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the HotLinks mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name and email address
  • Profile photos
  • Date of birth (to verify age)
  • Gender and gender preferences

Profile Information

  • Bio and interests
  • Favorite date spots
  • Budget preferences
  • Availability schedule

Location Data

  • GPS coordinates when using the "Go Live" feature
  • General location for matching purposes (fuzzed to ~1km precision before storage)
  • Venue check-ins

Usage Data

  • App interactions and preferences
  • Device information
  • Crash reports and diagnostics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To match you with compatible users
  • To enable live location sharing features
  • To send notifications about matches and dates
  • To ensure safety and prevent fraud
  • To analyze and improve app performance

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only:

  • With other users (profile info you make public)
  • With service providers who help operate our app (Firebase, Google Cloud, RevenueCat, Sentry, Google AdMob)
  • When required by law
  • To protect safety or prevent fraud

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication via Firebase Auth
  • Server-side access controls and document-level security rules
  • Regular security audits

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your data (Settings → Export My Data)
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Control location sharing preferences

6. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. After account deletion, we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use analytics tools (Firebase Analytics, Sentry) to understand app usage and improve our services. You can control analytics sharing in your in-app privacy settings.

8. Advertising

Free-tier users see ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use your device identifier to deliver relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings, or upgrade to HotLinks Ultra to remove ads entirely.

9. Children's Privacy

HotLinks is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. Account creation requires age verification, and accounts with age under 18 are blocked at the server level.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:
Email: privacy@hotlinks.app
